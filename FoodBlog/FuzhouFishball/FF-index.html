Fuzhou Fish Ball

Fuzhou Fish Ball is a traditional local dish in Fuzhou City, Fujian Province, belonging to the Fujian cuisine system. Thisdish has natural, nutritious, and healthy culinary characteristics. It has a good taste, good muscle strength, long cooking without spoilage,good swelling rate, white and exquisite color, tender, smooth and crispy texture, rich elasticity, delicious taste, and special seafood flavor. This dish is rich in protein, calcium, phosphorus, iodine, iron, and various vitamins. Introduction to Fuzhou Fish ballis a ball shaped food made from eel, shark, or mackerel minced, mixed with sweet potato powder(starch), and then wrapped with pork lean meat or shrimp filling. It is one of the local specialties of Fuzhou.

Fuzhou is a famous hometown of overseas Chinese, with over 4.3 million overseas Chinese of Fuzhou nationality scattered overseas. How can overseas Chinese and foreign friends eat Fuzhou fish balls?
