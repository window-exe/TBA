Kampar Amazing Dessert- Ding Feng Tau Fu Fa

Tofu is a traditional Chinese food known all over the world. There are many different varieties of tofu and Tau Fu Fa is one of the famous dishes of it. Tau Fu Fa is a very popular Chinese dessert made with very soft tofu that can be eaten either hot or cold. It is made by coagulated soy milk, served with syrup infused with ginger, brown sugar or pandan. Tau Fu Fa is a healthy food that has tons of nutritional value. In the old days, it was sold by street vendors but nowadays it can found it as part of the dim sum offering in Chinese restaurant.

Value Proposition
Kampar Ding Feng Tou Fu Fa is the main brunch of the Ding Feng Tau Fu Fa. The director of Ding Feng Tou Fu Fa is a lecturer of UTAR Kampar. He is operating his business not only in Kampar New Town but also in UTAR cafeteria and Ipoh. Ding Feng Tau Fu Fa is a shop that mainly selling all the dishes about soya bean. The 6 dishes selling in the shop are Traditional Tau Fu Fa that can choose the syrup flavor ourselves, Soy milk, Tau Fu Fa with soy milk, Soy milk with Sea Bird’s Nest topping, Soy milk with jelly, Tau Fu Fa with Sea Bird’s Nest and Ginkgo Nuts topping. In the day we go to visit Ding Feng Tau Fu Fa, we decide to try all the dishes because we got 6 people and all of us can try the dishes together.
