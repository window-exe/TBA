Delicious Taiping Food: A One-Day Food Hunt In Taiping

Taiping,a town in Perak, is home to many tourist's attractions, such as Lake Garden, Maxwell Hill, Zoo Taiping, Taiping Railway station, and Perak Museum. Although the local delicious in Taiping may not be as famous as Ipoh, but there are many food places worth visiting.

Thus, in this artical, we will be exploring the delicious local food in Taiping. In this small town, the authenticity and tastes of the local delicoius willl never disappoint. We guarantee that a one-day food hunt in Taiping will satisfy your taste buds and tummy.

A good restaurant is like a vacation; it transports you, and it becomes a lot more than just about the food.
By Philip Rosenthal

1. Taiping PSL Goreng Pisang

P.S.L. Goreng Pisang is a very famous pisang goreng (fried banana fritters) seller in Taiping. The shop is located along Jalan Pasar, near where the goldsmiths and jewelers are congregated. This is a very old and established business in Taiping, having been around for more than 20 years now.

2. Restoran Kakak

If you enjoy noodles in clear soup, this restaurant is the best place to visit. Known for serving shredded chicken noodles, Restoran Kakak attracts new and returning cuatomer every day Each bowl of noodles comes with a rich chicken brith, springy hor fun noodles, and a generous amount of shredded chicken.

There is also a variety of sides and toppings to enjoy with your noodles. Some examples are pork balls, fish balls, and dumplings. The signature drink of this restaurant, Jia Jia, is also worth mentioning. It has coffee mixed with barley juice and comes in hot(Jia Jia Re) or cold(Jia Jia Ping).

4. Antong Coffee Mill

The main reason to come here is the Aun Tong House: the building next to modern-day Antong’s Coffee Mill was the former Taiping home of Sun Yat-sen, first provisional President of the Republic of Ch ina and first leader of the Kuomintang, and his partner Chen Cuifen.

The Aun Tong House today functions as an office, but still preserves some old furniture, TP-images of Sun Yat-sen, and a beautifully carved wooden partition separating the home’s two main rooms. You can acce ss it from Antong Coffee Mill’s main shop area, where you’ll also find a free flow of the local coffee produc ts to coy visitors into purchasing the local beans. Don’t be greedy and buy at least a pack of the excellent coffee on sale here.

Address:8A, Jalan Asam Kumbang, Kampung Asam Kumbang, 34000 Taiping, Perak

5. Lian Thong Restaurant(Taiping)

One of the oldest coffee shop restaurants in Taiping is Lian Thong Restaurant. This restaurant offers a lot of selection for noodle dishes, rice dishes, beverages, ad fried delicacies.

The hottest order in this restaurant is the Taufu Pop. It has been curds stuffed with seasoned, minced meat and deep-fried uuntil crispy and fragrant. Kerabu rice is also a dish worth trying. It consists of onions, prawn crackers, fried rice, and either fish or chicken. Noodle lovers can choose Yee Mee and Jawa noodles.
