Second Street (二街) Hawker Centre

Pengkalan Street Hawker Centre, or more well known to local as Second Street (二街) can be equated as Gurney Drive hawker centre Sungai Petani version, as it houses a variety of great yet inexpensive hawker food. There are perhaps about 30 hawkers from various ethnics - Malay, Chinese, Indian here, and exodus of crowd can be seen flocking here during dinner time. Few stalls are open during lunch hours though.

Chicken Chop & Pancake @ Eupe Food Court

There's a popular food court in Sungai Petani called Eupe Food Court, also known as 28 Shops. It's located in Taman Ria Jaya and serves a variety of delicious foods such as fish and pork curry noodles, wonton noodles, pan mee, Western food, and fried Jawa mee. This time, Applefish wants to introduce the Western food stall that has been in business for over 20 years. If you visit this food court, the must-try Western dishes are the chicken chop and pancake.
