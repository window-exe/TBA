Fuzhou Fish Ball

Creator - Jiang Cheng Jun

Fuzhou Fish Ball is a traditional local dish in Fuzhou City, Fujian Province, belonging to the Fujian cuisine system. Thisdish has natural, nutritious, and healthy culinary characteristics. It has a good taste, good muscle strength, long cooking without spoilage,good swelling rate, white and exquisite color, tender, smooth and crispy texture, rich elasticity, delicious taste, and special seafood flavor. This dish is rich in protein, calcium, phosphorus, iodine, iron, and various vitamins. Introduction to Fuzhou Fish ballis a ball shaped food made from eel, shark, or mackerel minced, mixed with sweet potato powder(starch), and then wrapped with pork lean meat or shrimp filling. It is one of the local specialties of Fuzhou.

Sungai Petani Food

Creator - Yap Chee How

Hello there! My name is YAP CHEE HOW, and I am a true food aficionado hailing from the charming town of Sungai Petani in Kedah, Malaysia. Located in the heart of the northern region of the country, Sungai Petani is a hidden gem that boasts a rich cultural heritage and a delectable array of mouth-watering cuisine. I am incredibly proud of my roots, and I am excited to share with you the unique and scrumptious food offerings that our town has to offer. Growing up in Sungai Petani, I was exposed to an eclectic mix of culinary delights that were influenced by the town's diverse ethnic communities. This has led me to develop a deep appreciation for the various tastes and flavors that have shaped my palate and fueled my passion for food.
